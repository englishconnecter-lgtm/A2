Stage 1 — Picture warm-up
What is happening?
7 minutes · Speaking and prediction

Arranging a meeting by phone

Look at the picture. Who is the person calling? What meeting does he need to arrange?

Discuss:

1. Do you often arrange meetings at work or school?

2. Is it easier to arrange a meeting by phone, message, or email?

3. What words do you need? Think of days, times, places, and polite questions.
