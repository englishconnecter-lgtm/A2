Stage 1 — Warm-up
A simple business idea
5 minutes · speaking

Start a small business

In this lesson, students talk about a simple business idea, learn useful vocabulary, practise grammar for explaining ideas, and finish with a short pitch.

Discuss with a partner:

1. Would you like to have a small business? Why / why not?

2. What small businesses are popular in your town?

3. What do you need to start a small business?
