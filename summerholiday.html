Page 4 — Grammar

Comparatives: cheaper, hotter, more expensive

10 minutes · Rules and practice

Short adjectives

cheap → cheaper
hot → hotter
long → longer

Use: Lisbon is cheaper than Kyoto.

Long adjectives

expensive → more expensive
interesting → more interesting

Use: Kyoto is more expensive than Lisbon.

Make 4 sentences:

1. Lisbon / cheap / Kyoto

2. Kyoto / hot / Costa Rica

3. Costa Rica / far / Lisbon

4. Kyoto / expensive / Lisbon
